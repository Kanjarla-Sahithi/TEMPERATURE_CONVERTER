Do you want to convert your celsius temperature to fahrenheit or fahrenheit
temperature to celsius? Then here is the formula

If you want to find out the fahrenheit temperature which is in the celsius then
the formula is
Temp in degrees Fahrenheit (°F) = (Temp in degrees Celsius (°C) * 9/5) + 32.

If you want to find out the celsius temperature which is in the fahrenheit then
the formula is
Temp in degrees Celsius (°C) = (Temp in degrees Fahrenheit (°F) - 32) * 5/9.
